# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** May 7, 2026

## 1. Who this policy is from

This privacy policy applies to the **softly** mobile application ("the app"), published by Farbod Faramarzi as an individual developer ("we", "us"). Contact: fabbe@suprawsm.com.

## 2. The short version

The app does not collect, transmit, sell, share, or analyze any data about you or the person whose stuttering you are tracking. It contains no analytics, no advertising, no third-party tracking, no telemetry, and no user accounts. Everything you enter stays on your device. Optional iCloud synchronization is provided by Apple's standard system service and is configured so that only your own Apple ID has access.

If that is all you wanted to know, you can stop reading.

## 3. What the app stores

When you use softly, the following information is saved on your device:

- Daily severity scores you log (a number from 0 to 10 with the date).
- Free-text notes you choose to write, organized by week.
- Your in-app settings (notification preferences, reminder time, language).
- A flag indicating that you have accepted the in-app disclaimer.

That is the complete list. The app does not store names, ages, photos, contact details, location, device identifiers, IP addresses, account credentials, health metrics from other sources, or anything else.

The data is not tied to a person, an identity, or a profile. We do not ask for, generate, or attach any identifier. The app has no concept of "user account" and no way to associate the data on your device with a specific individual.

## 4. Where the data lives

All data is stored locally on your device inside the app's sandboxed container, using the operating system's standard storage facility (`AsyncStorage` on iOS).

If you have iCloud enabled on your device, the data is also written to your personal iCloud key-value storage. This is a feature of iCloud that lets the same app installed on your other devices, signed into the same iCloud account, read back the data you have entered. The purpose of this is solely to:

- let you continue tracking on a second device of yours (for example a tablet),
- and let you recover your data if you lose, replace, or reinstall the app on your phone.

Your iCloud data is encrypted and managed entirely by Apple. We have no access to it. We do not operate any server, database, or backend. Nothing about your usage of the app reaches us, ever.

If you do not want iCloud sync, you can disable iCloud for softly in your device's iCloud settings, or sign out of iCloud entirely. The app will continue to work using local storage.

## 5. What we do not do

To be explicit:

- We do **not** collect analytics, usage statistics, crash reports, or any kind of telemetry.
- We do **not** show advertisements.
- We do **not** track you across apps or websites.
- We do **not** share, sell, rent, license, or transfer any data to third parties.
- We do **not** integrate any third-party SDKs that collect data on our behalf.
- We do **not** use cookies or similar technologies (the app is not a website).
- We do **not** profile users, build behavioral models, or train machine-learning systems on your data.

The App Store privacy label for this app reads "Data Not Collected", which is accurate.

## 6. Notifications

If you enable the daily reminder, the app schedules a local notification on your device using the operating system's notification system. The reminder text and timing are stored on your device only. No notification content is sent through any push notification server.

## 7. Children

This app is intended to be used by an adult (typically a parent or legal guardian) to record observations about a child receiving speech therapy. The app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The data entered describes severity ratings only and contains no identifying information about the child. The adult using the app is solely responsible for any free-text notes they choose to write and for ensuring the device on which the app is installed is appropriate for storing such notes.

## 8. Your rights

Because we hold no data about you on any server, we cannot identify you, locate your data, or provide you with copies of it. You retain full control of your data on your own device. You can:

- View and edit any entry directly in the app.
- Delete all your data at any time by uninstalling the app.
- Disable iCloud sync at any time from your device settings.

If you uninstall the app and have iCloud sync enabled, the iCloud copy may persist until iCloud removes it according to its own policies; you can delete it manually from iCloud storage management on your device.

## 9. No medical or clinical use

softly is a personal journal. It is not a medical device, does not provide diagnosis, treatment, or clinical guidance, and should be used only under the supervision of a qualified speech-language pathologist. By using the app you accept that the developer takes no responsibility for how data entered into the app is interpreted or acted upon. Decisions about a child's care belong with the treating therapist.
