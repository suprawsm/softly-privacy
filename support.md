## Common questions

### My data disappeared after reinstalling
softly stores your data locally on your device. If you have iCloud enabled for softly, your scores and notes are also synced to your personal iCloud account and should restore automatically when you reinstall the app and sign in with the same Apple ID. If iCloud was disabled, uninstalling the app removes the data permanently.

### How do I move my data to a new phone?
Make sure iCloud is enabled for softly on both devices and that both devices are signed into the same Apple ID. Your data syncs through Apple's iCloud key-value store automatically.

### How do I disable the daily reminder?
Open softly → Settings → turn off "Notifications". You can also change the reminder time from the same screen.

### How do I change the app's language?
Open softly → Settings → Language → pick System, English, or Svenska.

### Is softly a medical device?
No. softly is a personal journal for tracking daily stuttering severity scores. It is not a diagnostic or treatment tool and should be used only under the guidance of a qualified speech-language pathologist. See the privacy policy and in-app disclaimer for details.

### Does softly collect any data about me?
No. softly does not collect, transmit, or share any data. Everything stays on your device (and optionally in your personal iCloud). See the [privacy policy](./privacy) for the full details.
